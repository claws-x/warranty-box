# WarrantyBox Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: 2026-04-04

## Overview

WarrantyBox values your privacy. This app is designed to help you manage warranty records, reminder settings, and receipt images on your device.

## Information We Handle

WarrantyBox may store the following information locally on your device:

- Product names
- Purchase dates
- Warranty durations
- Store names
- Notes you enter
- Receipt or product images you choose
- Reminder preferences

## How The Information Is Used

The information above is used only to provide core app features, including:

- displaying and organizing your warranty items
- calculating expiration dates
- scheduling local reminder notifications
- showing receipt images that you attach to an item

## Data Storage

WarrantyBox stores app data locally on your device. The app does not automatically upload your warranty records to a remote server.

## Permissions

WarrantyBox may request the following permissions:

- Notifications: used to send local reminders before a warranty expires
- Camera: used to capture a receipt or product image
- Photo Library: used to select an existing receipt or product image

You can change permission settings at any time in the iOS Settings app.

## Third-Party Sharing

WarrantyBox does not sell your personal information. Based on the current version of the app, warranty data is not automatically shared with third parties.

## Children's Privacy

WarrantyBox is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
